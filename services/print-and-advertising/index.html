Print design and advertising.

Brochures, sales literature, press advertising, direct mail and corporate print designed to reflect the quality of your brand. Southampton and Hampshire.

Print that works

Physical presence in a digital world.

Print is not dead. For certain audiences, in certain contexts, a beautifully produced brochure or a well-designed advertisement makes a stronger impression than any digital equivalent. There is a physicality to high-quality print that conveys permanence, substance and investment -- qualities that matter enormously when you are trying to sell a premium product, attract a high-value client or position a business at the quality end of its market.

At Now Marketing, print design has been at the heart of our work since the agency was founded in 2003. We have designed sales brochures for residential developments, trade publications for manufacturers, corporate reports for professional services firms and direct mail campaigns for businesses across Hampshire and the South. In every case, the same principle applies: print design should reflect the quality of the business it represents.

We handle the complete process: strategy and concept, copywriting, design, artwork and liaison with our trusted print suppliers. We understand paper stocks, finishes, binding methods and print specifications, which means we can advise on the production choices that will achieve the look and feel you are after within your budget, without the costly mistakes that come from not knowing the technical side.

What we produce

A full range of print and advertising work.

Brochures and Sales Literature

Residential development brochures, corporate capabilities documents, product catalogues, annual reports and service guides. We design and produce print literature that communicates your offer clearly and reflects your brand at its best. From a four-page leaflet to a sixty-page specification document, the same care goes into every piece.

Press and Outdoor Advertising

Newspaper and magazine advertisements, outdoor poster campaigns, billboard and site hoarding design. We create advertising that demands attention within the space available and delivers the message clearly and memorably. Property advertising, in particular, is a specialism we have developed through years of working with leading Hampshire agents.

Direct Mail

Direct mail remains one of the most effective channels for reaching specific target audiences with a considered message. We design direct mail campaigns that stand out in the post, respect the intelligence of the recipient and give them a compelling reason to act. From simple postcards to multi-piece packs, we handle design, production and print management throughout.

Corporate Stationery and Print

Business cards, letterheads, compliment slips, presentation folders and internal communication materials. Corporate print is often undervalued but it communicates professionalism at every touchpoint where your business makes contact with clients or prospects. We design stationery that is consistent with your brand and produced to a quality that reflects well on your business.

Print and digital integration

Print that works alongside your digital presence.

The most effective marketing programmes integrate print and digital consistently. A property brochure that reflects the same visual language as the development website. A direct mail piece that drives traffic to a dedicated landing page. A press advertisement that reinforces a social media campaign. When print and digital are aligned, each channel makes the other stronger.

Because we work across both print and digital, we are well placed to ensure this consistency. We design your brochure with the same creative thinking that shapes your website, and we brief photographers and copywriters with both channels in mind so that the assets produced serve every medium. This joined-up approach saves time and budget and produces a more coherent brand experience for your audience.

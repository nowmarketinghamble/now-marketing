Exhibition design Hampshire

Exhibition design and signage.

Exhibition stands, pop-up displays, office signage, wayfinding and vehicle livery for businesses across Hampshire and the South of England.

Physical brand presence

Standing out in the real world.

Brand presence is not just digital. At trade shows and exhibitions, in your office reception, on your vehicles and your building, how you present your brand in the physical world makes a powerful statement about the quality and professionalism of your business. An exhibition stand that looks tired or generic, or signage that does not reflect your brand accurately, undermines the impression you make on the people who matter most.

We design exhibition stands, pop-up systems, office signage, wayfinding systems and vehicle livery for businesses across Hampshire and the South. Our approach is the same as for every other medium: start with the brand, understand the objective, and design something that achieves the right outcome in the most direct and impactful way.

We have extensive experience in designing for the constraints of physical display: the viewing distances, the ambient light conditions, the need for clarity at a glance, the importance of hierarchy when a visitor has seconds to absorb the key message. Whether you are briefing an eight-metre trade show installation or a roller banner for a local networking event, we bring the same level of thought to the outcome.

We work with trusted suppliers across Hampshire and the South to handle print production and installation, and we manage the whole process on your behalf. You receive artwork and, if required, a managed installation with nothing left to chance.

What we design and produce

Exhibitions, signage and livery.

01
Exhibition Stands

Modular and bespoke exhibition stand design for trade shows, corporate events and sector exhibitions. We design for maximum flexibility across different stand sizes, with strong visual hierarchy and clear messaging at every scale. From concept through to production-ready artwork and supplier liaison.

02
Pop-Up Displays

Roller banners, pop-up stands, tension fabric displays and portable display systems. Designed to create a strong brand presence in environments ranging from conference lobbies to local business events. Practical, portable and visually impactful.

03
Office Signage

Reception signage, wall graphics, meeting room identification, branded window vinyl and external fascia signage. Your office environment communicates your brand values to everyone who visits -- clients, candidates and suppliers. We design signage that makes the right impression from the moment someone walks through the door.

04
Wayfinding

Direction and information signage systems for larger premises, business parks, retail environments and public spaces. Wayfinding design that is clear, accessible and consistent with your brand identity, designed to guide people through your environment without confusion or frustration.

05
Vehicle Livery

Van and fleet vehicle livery design for businesses operating across Hampshire and the South. Your vehicles are mobile advertisements -- properly designed livery turns every journey into a brand-building opportunity. We design livery that is visually strong, legally compliant and consistent with your wider brand identity.

06
Site Hoardings

Construction site and development hoardings designed to communicate the project and the developer brand while the work is underway. Particularly relevant for our property development clients, where hoarding design is an important first impression for potential purchasers.

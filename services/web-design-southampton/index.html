Web design that works harder.

Your website is the most important marketing asset your business owns. It is working for you every hour of every day: answering questions, forming impressions, generating enquiries or failing to. The difference between a website that converts visitors into customers and one that simply takes up space on the internet comes down to two things: how it looks, and how it works.

At Now Marketing, we design websites from a UX perspective first. That means we start every project by thinking carefully about who your visitors are, what they need to find, and what action you want them to take. The visual design -- as important as it is -- is built on that foundation. Beautiful design that confuses or frustrates users is expensive decoration. Design that guides, informs and converts is an investment.

Every site we design is mobile-first. That is not a selling point; it is a baseline requirement. More than half of all web traffic now comes from mobile devices, and Google's ranking algorithm prioritises mobile performance. Designing for desktop first and adapting down invariably produces a compromised mobile experience. Designing for mobile first produces a better outcome on every device.

We also design with performance in mind. Fast-loading pages rank better in search, reduce bounce rates and improve conversion. Accessibility compliance is built in from the start, not added as an afterthought. And every design we produce is structured for CMS integration, so your team can update content without needing a developer every time.

We work with businesses across Hampshire and the South of England -- from Southampton and Portsmouth to Winchester, Romsey, Fareham, Andover and beyond. Whatever the size or sector of your business, we bring the same level of care to every project we take on.

Our design process

From brief to live in five stages.

01
Discovery

Understanding your business, your users, your competitors and your goals. We document requirements thoroughly before any design begins.

02
Wireframes

Low-fidelity layouts that define page structure, content hierarchy and user flow. Agreed before any visual design is applied, to keep revisions fast.

03
Design

Full-fidelity visual designs, desktop and mobile. We present the design thinking behind every decision and iterate collaboratively until perfect.

04
Build

Pixel-perfect front-end build. Clean, semantic code. CMS integration. Performance and accessibility testing throughout the build phase.

05
Launch

Pre-launch QA across devices and browsers. Analytics setup. Search console verification. A confident, well-prepared launch -- not a fingers-crossed one.

Common questions

Frequently asked questions.

How long does a website design project take?

A typical website design project takes between six and twelve weeks from initial brief to launch, depending on the size and complexity of the site. We agree a clear timeline at the outset and keep you updated throughout. Projects stay on track when both sides respond promptly at each review stage.

Do you offer website hosting?

Yes. We can arrange hosting and domain management on your behalf, or work with your existing hosting provider if you have one. We recommend reliable managed hosting environments that offer good performance and security, and we can advise on the best option for your site's requirements.

Do you build websites on WordPress?

WordPress is our most commonly used CMS because it is genuinely excellent for most business websites: flexible, well-supported, and straightforward for clients to manage. We also build on other platforms and in bespoke code when the project calls for it. We will recommend what is right for your project, not just what we default to.

How do I get started?

The simplest starting point is a conversation. Call us on 07899 792663 or send a message via the contact form. We will ask a few questions about what you are looking to achieve, give you a clear idea of cost and timescale, and provide a written proposal if you would like to go ahead.

Will my website work on mobile?

Yes, absolutely. Every website we design is mobile-first and fully responsive across all devices and screen sizes. We test across a wide range of devices and browsers before launch, and we do not consider a project complete until it looks and performs correctly everywhere your users will encounter it.

Do you offer ongoing support after launch?

Yes. We offer ongoing support, maintenance and content update packages. We can also provide training so your team can make day-to-day updates themselves. Either way, we are always available to help if something needs attention after your site goes live.

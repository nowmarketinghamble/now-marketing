What is PPC?

Immediate visibility for high-intent searches.

Pay-per-click advertising is a model where you pay only when someone clicks your ad. In practice, it means your business can appear at the top of Google search results for specific terms from day one, reaching people who are actively searching for what you offer at the moment they are looking for it. Managed well, PPC delivers a measurable, predictable return. Managed badly, it burns budget with little to show for it.

The difference between effective PPC management and wasteful PPC management comes down to precision. The right keywords, the right match types, the right ad copy, the right landing pages, the right bidding strategy and rigorous ongoing optimisation. It also requires honest reporting: knowing exactly what your ad spend is producing, not just impressions and clicks, but actual enquiries, sales and revenue.

We manage PPC campaigns for businesses across Southampton, Hampshire and the South with a singular focus on return on investment. We do not set up campaigns and leave them running. We monitor performance closely, test new approaches continuously, and make the kind of marginal improvements that compound into significantly better outcomes over time.

Platforms we manage

The right platform for your audience.

Google Ads

Search campaigns, Shopping ads, Display network, YouTube and Performance Max. Google Ads is the dominant paid search platform and the first choice for capturing demand from people who are actively searching for your products or services. We manage every aspect of your Google Ads account, from keyword selection and ad copy to bidding strategies and landing page recommendations. Our focus is always on cost per conversion, not cost per click.

Meta Advertising

Facebook and Instagram ads for awareness, engagement and conversion. Meta's advertising platform offers unparalleled audience targeting based on demographics, interests, behaviours and lookalike audiences. It is particularly effective for building brand awareness, driving consideration among relevant audiences, and retargeting people who have already visited your website. We create campaigns that are visually compelling and strategically targeted to the audiences most likely to convert.

Campaign types

The right campaign for your objective.

Search Campaigns

Text ads shown when people search specific terms on Google. The most direct way to capture high-intent demand at the moment of search. Ideal for service businesses and lead generation.

Display Campaigns

Visual banner ads shown across Google's display network. Excellent for building brand awareness and retargeting website visitors who did not convert on their first visit.

Shopping Campaigns

Product listing ads that show product images, prices and retailer information directly in search results. Essential for e-commerce businesses selling physical products online.

Remarketing

Ads shown specifically to people who have previously visited your website. Remarketing converts undecided visitors into customers by keeping your brand in front of them during the consideration period.

Social Advertising

Paid campaigns on Facebook, Instagram and LinkedIn. Highly targetable by audience demographics, interests and behaviour. Excellent for awareness campaigns and targeting specific professional audiences.

Local Campaigns

Google Ads and social campaigns targeting specific geographic areas. Particularly valuable for Southampton and Hampshire businesses targeting local customers within a defined radius.

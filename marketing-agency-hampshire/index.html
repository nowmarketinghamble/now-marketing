Hampshire is one of the most commercially diverse counties in the South of England. From the city economy of Southampton to the market towns of Winchester, Alresford and Petersfield, from the commuter belt of Basingstoke to the coastal communities of Fareham and Lee-on-the-Solent -- it is a county with real economic breadth. Serving it well as a marketing agency requires genuine understanding of its different markets and audiences.

Now Marketing is based in Netley Abbey, within the Southampton city boundary, and we work with businesses right across Hampshire. Our clients include property developers, estate agents, professional services firms, hospitality businesses, local retailers and B2B companies. Some of them are household names within Hampshire. Others are ambitious smaller businesses that want marketing of a quality that reflects their ambitions.

What sets us apart is the combination of creative quality and strategic clarity. We do not produce marketing for marketing's sake. Everything we do is grounded in a clear understanding of what a business is trying to achieve, who it is trying to reach, and what will actually move those people to act. That sounds obvious, but in practice it requires a level of rigour that many agencies skip in favour of generating impressive-looking output.

Our founder and creative director, Mike Lewis, has worked with Hampshire businesses for more than two decades. His work spans brand identity, web design, print, advertising, SEO and digital marketing. That breadth of experience means we can give genuinely joined-up advice across all of these disciplines, rather than pushing clients towards whatever service happens to be most profitable for the agency that month.

Why choose a Hampshire agency

We know the Hampshire market because we work in it every day.

Hampshire has a particular commercial character that national agencies based in London or Manchester do not always understand. The county has a significant number of premium and affluent consumers -- particularly in Winchester, the Meon Valley, the New Forest and the South Downs -- which means the bar for quality in marketing is often higher than it might be elsewhere. At the same time, it has a thriving community of ambitious smaller businesses that do not have large marketing budgets but want results that are proportionate to their investment.

We have spent more than two decades working with Hampshire businesses across both of those groups. We understand the local property market, the professional services landscape, the retail and hospitality sector, and the B2B economy that underpins much of the county's commercial activity. That local knowledge informs everything we do, from the way we approach a brand strategy to the keywords we target in an SEO campaign.

We are also well positioned geographically. Our studio in Netley Abbey puts us within easy reach of Southampton, Fareham, Eastleigh and Winchester -- and we are always happy to travel further afield within the county for client meetings, site visits and project presentations. Good client relationships are built face to face, and we believe in that.

For businesses outside Hampshire who have found their way to this page -- we work nationally too. Many of our clients are based outside the county and work with us remotely. The quality of the work is the same wherever you are.

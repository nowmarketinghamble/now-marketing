Our property expertise

A specialist in property marketing.

Charters Strutt & Parker Wykeham Homes St Cross Homes Doswell Newman Cozens

Property marketing sits at an interesting intersection of disciplines. The best estate agents and residential developers understand that their brand is not just how they look -- it is the total impression they make at every touchpoint, from the agent's board at the end of the road to the brochure that a buyer takes home, to the website that appears when they search online the next morning.

Now Marketing has been working with property clients across Hampshire and the South Coast for over twenty years. Our clients include some of the most respected names in the regional property market: Charters, one of Hampshire's leading independent estate agencies; Strutt & Parker, whose regional operations we have supported with design and marketing work; Wykeham Homes and St Cross Homes, quality Hampshire developers whose sales literature and marketing we have produced; and established names including Doswell and Newman Cozens.

What makes property marketing distinctive is its breadth. A successful property marketing programme requires consistent brand application across an unusual range of formats: from A1 hoardings to A5 brochure inserts, from portal photography to social media, from development brochures to press advertising. Maintaining quality and consistency across all of these simultaneously requires an agency that understands the medium, the audience and the pace at which property marketing moves.

We understand the property purchase journey and the role that marketing plays at each stage: building awareness of the development or agency, creating emotional connection with the product, providing the information buyers need to make a decision, and then reinforcing confidence through the sales process. Every piece of marketing we produce for property clients is designed with this journey in mind.

Services for property clients

What we do for the property sector.

Brand and Identity

Agency brand refreshes, new development naming and identity, developer brand guidelines. We have created and refreshed brands for both estate agents and residential developers at various scales, from boutique independents to multi-office regional networks.

Development Brochures

Sales brochures, specification documents and move-in packs for residential developments. Premium print quality that reflects the quality of the homes being sold, with clear floorplan presentation, lifestyle photography briefs and compelling copywriting throughout.

Website Design and Development

Agent and developer websites with property search integration, lead capture, development microsites and CMS management. Mobile-first, SEO-optimised and designed to convert visitors into enquiries. We understand the technical requirements of property portals and CRM integration.

Digital Marketing

SEO, PPC and social media campaigns for estate agents and developers. Local SEO is particularly important in the property sector, where buyers are specifically searching for properties and agents in defined geographic areas. We help property businesses appear prominently for the search terms that drive buyer enquiries.

Photography Briefs

We work alongside photographers and videographers on development photoshoots, briefing for the images and footage needed across digital, print and social channels. We cannot shoot the photography, but we can ensure the right brief is in place to capture what the marketing programme needs.

Sales Support Materials

Site hoardings, show home dressing graphics, display boards, agent particulars templates, signage and point-of-sale materials. Everything a development sales office or estate agency needs to present its product confidently to buyers throughout the sales process.
